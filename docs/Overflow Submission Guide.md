### Hi builders!

As we get closer to the Sui Overflow 2026 submission deadline on June 21st, here are a few quick tips to help you prepare a strong project submission.
And if you haven’t started yet, it’s still not too late to join the hackathon!

### What makes a strong submission?

A great submission clearly explains:
- What you built
- Why it matters
- How it works
- How you used the Sui stack

### Demo video guidance

Your demo video is especially important. We recommend:
- 30–60 sec: Problem overview
- ~3 min: Product demo
- 30–60 sec: Conclusion and future vision

We also encourage teams to showcase user testing, feedback, and MVP improvements throughout the build process.

### Quick checklist before submitting
✅ Demo video completed
✅ GitHub repo updated and public
✅ Clear presentation/demo flow
✅ All project details filled out

### Common mistakes to avoid
- Incomplete demos
- Missing documentation/private repo links
- Not explaining the product value or Sui integration clearly

Good luck! We’re excited to see what you create!
