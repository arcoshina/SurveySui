# SurveySui is a survey reward platform on the Sui blockchain

## Why run surveys on a blockchain?

- **Instant reward payout**: the survey budget is locked into an on-chain vault when the survey is created, and the payout rules are written into a smart contract, so the creator cannot back out afterward.
- **Zero barrier to answer**: respondents don't need to buy tokens first; even a wallet with a 0 balance can complete a survey, with the gas fee sponsored by the creator.
- **Both questions and answers can be encrypted**: your answers are encrypted in the browser before they are sent, so only the survey creator can decrypt and read them — neither the platform nor anyone else can see the content.

## I want to answer surveys — what do I need?

A Sui wallet.
If you have never used a crypto wallet, **Slush** is a good start: you can create a wallet and answer surveys using a social OAuth account (such as a Google account) first, and learn about web3 gradually later (though we don't recommend putting it off too long).

Respondents **don't need to buy tokens or deposit funds in advance**. After connecting a wallet, your early activity on the platform can have its gas subsidized by the platform or the survey creator.

## How to answer a survey and claim a reward

1. Check the eligibility requirements on the survey page.
2. Connect your wallet.
3. Complete identity verification (requirements vary by survey; we recommend binding Google, which also binds your Gmail).
4. Fill in and submit your answer; the gas is paid by the creator. Note: *do not enter private information!*
5. The reward tokens are automatically transferred to your wallet.

## I want to create surveys

If you want to run a reward survey, you'll need to fully understand how it works. Start with "Create a Survey" in the document list on the left.

## Current status

The platform is still under development and runs on testnet, for functional validation and demonstration. Testnet tokens currently have no real value.
Please follow our announcements for the official launch date.
